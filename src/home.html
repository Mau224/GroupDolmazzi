I am not sure I can express in words how much it feels like home, I just know I could not call this place mine without Dolmazzi’s devoted support. Thank you!

Objective: Sell a low-rise condominium with maximum revenue

The time had come to expand my family, and that meant moving on from our faithful abode. After much researching of agencies, we reached out to Anna and asked her to put our property on the market. I had my dream price, just as every seller does, though this was somewhat reined back to reality after discussing the comparables and market stats with Anna. I had done a stylish remodel, which featured everything from designer tiles from handmade faucets, and my condo was looking at her best. We sold the property to a wonderful buyer and, thanks to the guys and gals at Dolmazzi, the whole process was painless. For the first time in many years, we were back to house hunting, though thanks to Anna we weren’t at it long. Now, again with the help of Anna, we are enjoying our new home and watching our family grow into it.
