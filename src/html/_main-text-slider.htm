Located in the desirable El Dorado Park South region of Long Beach this completely renovated house features an open concept living area with hardwood flooring throughout.

The large kitchen features Carrera marble countertops, an island, Koville soft close cabinets, stainless steel appliances and Jeffrey Court beveled tile backsplash. The bathrooms feature floating vanities, custom shower glass, Jeffrey Court beveled tile, Kohler shower faucets, and polished tile. There are four spacious bedrooms that feature built in closets and modern ceiling fans. The fourth bedroom serves as an office/guest bedroom with custom built-in murphy bed and shelving. The interior of the house has central gas, A/C and upgraded electrical panel. The exterior of the house has been beautifully landscaped 360 degrees and features several seating areas in the large private backyard including a custom gazebo area and built-in gas fireplace. The yard also features professionally installed artificial turf for easy care maintenance and enjoyment in all weather conditions. The space has been designed for entertaining and can be enjoyed by all ages.
